  • 2023-2024 spring

    WHU Seminar: Dispersion of non-linear waves: geometry and frequency

    Textbook[Luk]&[Wang]

    Reading materials on space-time resonance[Germain]&[Pusateri]

    Syllabus: (1) Sketch of linear results; (2) Energy estimates, local well-posedness and blow-up criterion; (3) Elements of Lorentzian geometry, vector fields and multiplers; (4) Klainerman-Sobolev inequalities, global existence for $n \geq 4$ ; (5) Good derivatives, null condition and global existence for $n=3$ ; (6) Review of Littlewood-Paley theory and oscillatory integrals; (7) Non-endpoint Strichartz estimates, GWP for defocusing subcritical NLW; (8) Space-time resonance, relation to null condition.

    WHU Seminar: Paradifferential calculus and non-linear PDEs

    Textbook[Danchin] Vedio ExtraNote

    Syllabus: (1) Littlewood-Paley decomposition, Bernstein inequalities, Besov spaces; (2) Bony's paraproduct, paradifferential calculus; (3) Smoothness effect of heat flow, application for semilinear Schrödinger equations; (4) Fujita-Kato's global result of mild solutions to Navier-Stokes equations; (5) Transport equations, result from ODE, estimates of commutators; (6) Beale-Kato-Majda's blow-up criterion for Euler equations.
